about us

Our Salon

La Passion Hairdressing Salon is a place created especially for people who want full beauty. Our guests can always count on professional support, advice and help from the staff. A nice, intimate atmosphere as well as an individual approach to customers means that in our Salon you will find everything you need for complete relaxation.

As always, a Hairdressing Technician, I care about meeting the needs of each client. Years of apprenticeship under the watchful eye of the best stylists helped me establish cooperation with prestigious salons in Great Britain and, consequently, open the LA PASSION Salon. Our additional advantage is affordable prices for everyone.
